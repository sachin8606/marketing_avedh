Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Traffiqon ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you visit our website https://traffiqon.com, use our services, or interact with us.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with applicable Indian laws, GDPR, and other international privacy standards.

By accessing or using our website or services, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our website and services.

1. Interpretation and Definitions

1.1 Interpretation

Words with capitalized initials have specific meanings defined below. The definitions apply equally to singular and plural forms.

1.2 Definitions

  • Account: A unique profile created to access our Services.
  • Company: Traffiqon, located at Near Kargi Chowk, Dehradun, India – 248001.
  • Cookies: Small text files stored on your device that collect information about browsing activity.
  • Country: Refers to the Republic of India.
  • Device: Any device such as a desktop, laptop, mobile phone, or tablet that can access our Service.
  • Personal Data: Information that can identify an individual, directly or indirectly.
  • Service: Refers to all digital marketing services provided by Traffiqon, including SEO, GEO, GMB, Meta Ads, Google Ads, LinkedIn Ads, Performance Marketing, and related services.
  • Service Provider: Any third party engaged by Traffiqon to perform services or process data on our behalf.
  • Usage Data: Information collected automatically from the use of the Service (e.g., IP address, browser type, visit duration).
  • You/User: The individual or entity using our website or services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Data

When you use our website or services, we may collect the following information:

  • • Full Name

  • • Phone Number

  • • Address, State, City, and ZIP/Postal Code
  • • Any information you provide through forms, emails, or service inquiries

2.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect data such as:

  • • Your IP address and browser details
  • • Device information
  • • Time and duration of website visits
  • • Pages visited and interactions made

2.3 Data from Third-Party Integrations

If you log in or connect with us using platforms like Google, Facebook, or LinkedIn, we may access limited information from your social media account such as your name, email, and public profile details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your Personal Data to:

  • • Provide and maintain our services.
  • • Manage your account and communications.
  • • Process your service inquiries, campaigns, and contracts.
  • • Improve our website, marketing campaigns, and client experience.
  • • Send updates, promotions, or newsletters (only with your consent).
  • • Analyze website usage and performance for optimization.
  • • Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.
  • • Prevent fraud, security breaches, or misuse of our services.

4. Legal Basis for Processing Data (GDPR Compliance)

We process your personal data lawfully under the following grounds:

  • • You have given consent for specific purposes.
  • • Processing is necessary for contract performance.
  • • Processing is required for legal compliance.
  • • Processing is based on our legitimate business interests (e.g., service improvement, marketing analysis).

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use Cookies, Web Beacons, and Pixel Tags to enhance user experience and analyze performance.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website operation.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your choices (e.g., login info or language).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand site performance and visitor behavior.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used for retargeting and advertising (Google, Meta, LinkedIn, etc.).

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may limit some website features.

6. Sharing of Your Personal Data

We may share your data only under the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: Who assist us in analytics, hosting, email communication, advertising, and customer support.
  • For Business Transfers: If Traffiqon is merged or acquired, your data may be transferred to the new entity.
  • With Business Partners: For co-branded marketing or campaigns (only with your consent).
  • For Legal Reasons: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or lawful requests.
  • With Your Consent: For any other purpose that you approve.

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

7. Retention of Your Data

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to:

  • • Fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or
  • • Comply with legal, accounting, or reporting obligations.

Inactive account or data not required will be securely deleted or anonymized after 12 months of inactivity or upon your request.

8. Transfer of Data Internationally

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside India (such as the EU or the US) where our servers, partners, or vendors are located. We ensure such transfers comply with international data protection standards, ensuring your data remains secure and confidential.

9. Security of Your Data

We use industry-standard measures such as SSL encryption, firewalls, and secure access controls to protect your personal data.

However, no online transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We recommend you also take precautions when sharing sensitive information online.
